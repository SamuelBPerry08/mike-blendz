The Brand

I’m a young barber right here in Wilmington with a real passion for the craft. Mike Blendz started because I wanted to bring sharp, clean looks to my community without the huge price tag.

As a local small business owner, I take pride in every single fade and taper I do. I’m always staying on top of the newest styles to make sure you leave my chair feeling heard and looking like a new person. When you book with me, you’re not just getting a haircut, you’re supporting a local hustle.
